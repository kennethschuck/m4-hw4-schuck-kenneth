Doja Cat

Doja Cat is the best pop star currently in the music game right now. She has dominated the charts and influenced everyone who came after her. She is unstoppable and gets millions of streams every day.

History

Where did Doja Cat come from?

Doja Cat has been making music and posting it on Soundcloud since as early as 2012. But her rise to fame did not really accelerate until 2018 when she released her single called "MOOO!" which brought her to the coveted status of being a meme. Memes are the best way to promote music in the age of Generation Z. With the rise of TikTok, her easily accessible, catchy, pop-rap-r&b fusion took the world by storm.
